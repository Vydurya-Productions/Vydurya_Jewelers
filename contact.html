Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries about our jewelry and services

All Vydurya jewelry comes with a certificate of authenticity and BIS hallmark for gold purity. You can verify your purchase using the unique identification number on our website or by visiting any of our boutiques.

We offer a 15-day return policy for non-customized jewelry in its original condition. Custom pieces and engraved items cannot be returned or exchanged. All returns are subject to a 10% restocking fee.

Yes, our master artisans specialize in custom jewelry design. The process begins with a consultation (in-person or virtual) where we discuss your vision, followed by sketches, 3D renderings, and finally crafting your unique piece.

We recommend storing each piece separately in soft pouches, avoiding contact with chemicals and perfumes, and bringing your jewelry for professional cleaning at our boutiques every 6 months (complimentary for the first year).
