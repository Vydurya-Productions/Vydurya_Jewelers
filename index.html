Jewelry Care Essentials

Preserve the brilliance of your precious jewels

Cleaning

Use a soft cloth to gently polish your gold jewelry. For diamonds, use mild soapy water and a soft brush.

Storage

Store each piece separately in soft pouches to prevent scratches. Keep away from moisture and sunlight.

Maintenance

Bring your jewelry for professional cleaning and inspection every 6 months to maintain its luster.

Our Legacy of Craftsmanship

Founded in 1998, Vydurya Jewelers has been synonymous with exceptional craftsmanship and timeless designs. Our journey began in the heart of Mumbai with a small workshop and a big dream.

Today, we blend traditional Indian jewelry-making techniques with contemporary designs, creating pieces that are cherished across generations. Each jewel is a testament to our commitment to quality, purity, and artistry.
