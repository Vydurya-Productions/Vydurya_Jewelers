Discover Our Story

A Royal Heritage

Founded in the jewelry capital of India, Mumbai, Vydurya Jewelers began as a small family workshop crafting pieces for royal families. Our founder, Mr. Rajesh Vydurya, believed jewelry should tell a story - one of heritage, craftsmanship, and enduring beauty.

Today, we continue this tradition with master artisans trained in both traditional Indian techniques and contemporary design principles, creating pieces that transcend generations.

Our Journey

Milestones that shaped our legacy

1998

Humble Beginnings

Founded as a small workshop in Mumbai's Zaveri Bazaar, crafting custom pieces for local clientele.

2005

First Boutique

Opened our first retail boutique in South Mumbai, introducing the Vydurya brand to discerning customers.

2012

Royal Commission

Honored to create a bridal set for a royal wedding, marking our entry into high-end bespoke jewelry.

2018

National Recognition

Awarded 'Best Traditional Jewelry Designer' at the India Jewelry Awards.

2023

Digital Presence

Launched our e-commerce platform, bringing Vydurya craftsmanship to customers worldwide.
